The Film and Media Council (FMC) of IIT (BHU) is the institute's creative forum for film, media, and digital arts. Encompassing six clubs—Photography, Design, Cinematography, Media, Animation, and Social Outreach—it provides students with a platform to develop artistic and technical skills through workshops, competitions, and collaborative initiatives. With growing participation and consistent recognition at prestigious national events, FMC continues to enhance IIT (BHU)'s reputation in the fields of creative expression and digital innovation.
