The Science and Technology Council (SNTC) of IIT (BHU) serves as the hub for scientific innovation, technological advancement, and engineering excellence. Through its diverse clubs, the council provides students with platforms to explore cutting-edge technologies, participate in competitions, and develop skills that shape the future of science and engineering.
