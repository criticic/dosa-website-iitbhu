The Games and Sports Council (GNSC) of IIT (BHU) is dedicated to promoting physical fitness, sportsmanship, and athletic excellence among students. Through its various sports clubs and the annual Spardha festival, the council provides opportunities for students to engage in competitive sports, develop their athletic skills, and represent the institute at national and international levels.
