Sahyog, founded in 2017, is a student-led initiative at IIT (BHU) committed to enhancing the quality of education in government schools. With a mission to bridge the gap between government and private education, Sahyog promotes a practical and holistic approach to learning. Members regularly visit schools in Tikri, Sunderpur, and Adityanagar, teaching science, technology, and creative subjects through interactive sessions, experiments, and models - sparking curiosity, building confidence, and making education more engaging for young minds.
