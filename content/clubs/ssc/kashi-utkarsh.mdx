Kashi Utkarsh, the social service club of IIT (BHU), has been serving society since 1997 with a vision to uplift underprivileged communities. Focused on education, healthcare, and hygiene, the club works to extend learning opportunities and spread awareness among the most vulnerable. Through its initiatives, Kashi Utkarsh fosters dignity, confidence, and empowerment - driving students to practice empathy and contribute towards a more inclusive future.
