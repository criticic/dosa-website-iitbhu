Here, we entertain the complete spectrum of the wonders of language - be it humor, creativity, expression, and critical thinking through a variety of engaging activities. From poetry and story to spirited debates and witty word games, the club nurtures both the artistic and intellectual sides of its members. Regular events also include stand-up comedy and humorous improv, adding a fun twist to literary expression.
