DFZ is the official dance club of IIT BHU, serving as a vibrant hub for students passionate about dance. We embrace a multitude of dance forms, including contemporary, hip-hop, classical, and Bollywood, fostering an environment of creativity and expression. Our club is dedicated to nurturing talent, encouraging innovation, and promoting teamwork through regular practices, workshops, and performances.
