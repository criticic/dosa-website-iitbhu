At the Photography Club, IIT (BHU), we believe every frame tells a story. Our community of passionate creators explores the art and science of photography from capturing candid moments to experimenting with creative techniques and editing. Through workshops, photowalks, competitions, and collaborative projects, we provide a space for students to learn, share, and grow together. Whether you're picking up a camera for the first time or are already a skilled photographer, the club is where curiosity meets creativity, turning fleeting moments into timeless memories.
