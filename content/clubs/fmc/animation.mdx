The Animation Club is a dedicated platform for students passionate about creativity, visual storytelling, and digital design. The club focuses on producing high-quality 3D animations, visual effects (VFX), and artistic content using industry-standard tools such as Blender and other advanced software.

Our members collaborate to create imaginative projects, fantasy visuals, and innovative short films that showcase both technical expertise and artistic vision. Alongside creative pursuits, the club actively represents the institution in inter-college competitions, including the prestigious Inter IIT events, where we have consistently achieved commendable positions.
