The Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) Student Chapter at IIT (BHU) is committed to advancing automotive engineering through design, innovation, and competition. Through participation in events like BAJA, Formula Student, and Supra, workshops on vehicle dynamics and engine design, and industry collaborations, SAE provides students with hands-on experience in automotive engineering.
