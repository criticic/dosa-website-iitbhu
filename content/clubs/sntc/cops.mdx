The Club of Programmers (COPS) at IIT (BHU) is dedicated to promoting competitive programming, software development, and computer science excellence. Through coding contests, hackathons, workshops on algorithms and data structures, and participation in national and international competitions, COPS helps students sharpen their programming skills and problem-solving abilities.
